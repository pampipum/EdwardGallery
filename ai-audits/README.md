# Swiss AI Audit

Swiss-first workspace for selling AI audits and implementation services, starting in Davos and the canton of Graubünden.

## Market Focus

This project is no longer positioned as a generic global AI audit offer.

It is now a hyper-local Swiss consulting offer for:

- tourism and hospitality businesses
- boutique wealth managers and family-office style firms
- private clinics and specialist practices
- manufacturing and industrial KMUs

Primary geographic wedge:

- Davos
- Graubünden

Expansion geography:

- Bern
- Zürich

## Core Offer

### KI-Standortbestimmung

A compact, fixed-fee AI assessment for Swiss businesses that want clarity, not hype.

- price: `990 CHF`
- language: `Standard German first`, with English-ready variants
- focus: workflow review, tool fit, prioritization, and privacy-safe implementation guidance

## Swiss Positioning

The local angle matters:

- Swiss buyers are more privacy-sensitive
- decision-making is practical and risk-aware
- data handling trust is part of the sale
- the best language is sober, professional, and concrete

This repo therefore treats:

- `FADP / nDSG alignment`
- secure handling of interview transcripts and company data
- Swiss or European data residency
- restrained claims instead of hype

as core parts of the offer, not side notes.

## Compliance Notes

The sales page and report template explicitly reference:

- transparency obligations
- purpose limitation
- proportionality / data minimization
- secure processing
- access and deletion expectations

These are based on the revised Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection and official FDPIC guidance. They are positioning and process commitments, not legal advice.
