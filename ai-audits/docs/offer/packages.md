# Swiss Packages and Pricing

## Package 1

### KI-Standortbestimmung

Best for:

- SMEs
- hotels and hospitality groups
- private practices and clinics
- wealth management boutiques
- manufacturing KMUs

Scope:

- 45-minute discovery interview
- review of 3 to 5 high-friction workflows
- transcript-based analysis
- concrete tool and process recommendations
- 30-minute review session

Deliverables:

- executive summary
- priority matrix
- 3 to 7 recommendations
- quick wins for the next 7 to 30 days
- privacy and implementation notes

Turnaround:

- 3 to 5 business days

Price:

- `990 CHF`

## Package 2

### KI-Audit mit Umsetzungsfahrplan

Scope:

- everything in the assessment
- deeper workflow mapping
- scoring by impact, effort, and risk
- recommended tooling stack
- 30 / 60 / 90 day roadmap
- stakeholder presentation

Turnaround:

- 5 to 7 business days

Price:

- `2,900 CHF` to `5,900 CHF`

## Package 3

### Umsetzungs-Blueprint

Scope:

- future-state workflow design
- tool selection support
- ownership and rollout plan
- KPI design
- pilot definition

Price:

- `6,000 CHF` to `12,000 CHF`

## Upsell Ladder

- workflow redesign: `3,000 CHF` to `5,000 CHF`
- automation setup: `1,500 CHF` to `4,000 CHF`
- knowledge system build: `3,000 CHF+`
- custom prompt and template pack: `2,000 CHF` to `5,000 CHF`
- implementation sprint: `5,000 CHF` to `10,000 CHF+`

## Pricing Logic

- the `990 CHF` offer is the low-friction on-ramp
- the premium audit is the higher-trust, higher-signal offer
- implementation work is the real expansion layer

## Claims Discipline

Do not promise fixed hours saved or unconditional guarantees without delivery evidence.

Safer Swiss framing:

- identify realistic efficiency gains
- reduce process friction
- prioritize low-risk, high-value use cases
- improve operational clarity before implementation
