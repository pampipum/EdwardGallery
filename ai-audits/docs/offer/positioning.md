# Swiss Offer Positioning

## Flagship Entry Offer

### KI-Standortbestimmung

A fixed-scope AI assessment for Swiss businesses that need a realistic starting point for AI adoption.

It answers:

- where AI can create measurable value
- which workflows should be prioritized first
- which use cases should be avoided for now
- how to move forward without creating privacy or operational risk

## Market Thesis

Swiss SMEs and professional firms do not need another AI trend briefing.

They need:

- operational clarity
- privacy-safe recommendations
- sober ROI logic
- implementation options that fit a small team

The winning position is not "we do everything with AI."

It is:

`We assess your workflows, identify the most useful AI opportunities, and deliver a concrete plan that fits Swiss operational and privacy expectations.`

## Swiss Differentiators

- German-first communication with English-ready delivery
- CHF pricing and Swiss business tone
- explicit handling model for company data and transcripts
- references to Swiss data protection expectations under nDSG / FADP
- sector-specific examples for Davos, Graubünden, Bern, and Zürich
