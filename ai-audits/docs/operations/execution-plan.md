# Swiss Execution Plan

## Objective

Get from localized offer to first qualified conversations and first paid assessments in Davos and Graubünden.

## Phase 1: Swiss Positioning

Timeframe:

- days 1 to 3

Outputs:

- Swiss positioning
- CHF package structure
- sector-specific ICP
- German sales assets

Status:

- in progress

## Phase 2: Sales Assets

Timeframe:

- days 3 to 7

Build:

- German-first sales page
- German report template
- outreach sequences
- one-page assessment summary
- proposal template

Success criteria:

- you can send outreach without writing from scratch each time
- prospects can understand the offer in under 2 minutes

## Phase 3: Delivery Workflow

Timeframe:

- week 2

Build:

- intake questionnaire
- workflow interview template
- scoring rubric
- report template
- transcript analysis prompt
- privacy handling checklist

Success criteria:

- the audit can be delivered repeatably
- outputs look productized rather than improvised

## Phase 4: Early Distribution

Timeframe:

- weeks 2 to 4

Channel mix:

- warm intros in Graubünden
- local directory prospecting
- LinkedIn filtering by canton and sector
- targeted email
- referrals from agencies and digital service firms

Targets:

- 50 target accounts
- 25 personalized outbound messages
- 10 discovery conversations
- 3 paid assessments
- 1 implementation upsell

## 30-Day Priorities

1. Launch the German-first `990 CHF` KI-Standortbestimmung.
2. Build the target account list for Davos and Graubünden.
3. Validate privacy messaging against official Swiss guidance.
4. Start outreach by sector.
5. Convert first assessments into implementation work.

## What To Avoid

- promising hard ROI guarantees too early
- sounding like a generic AI agency
- ignoring privacy objections until late in the sales process
- starting with enterprise procurement-heavy accounts
- using US-style hype language

## Working Assumptions

- the first version should be sold manually
- local trust matters more than aggressive scale tactics
- repeatable German templates are the main leverage
- privacy posture is part of the product
