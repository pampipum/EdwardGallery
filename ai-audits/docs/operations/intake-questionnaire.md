# Audit Intake Questionnaire

## Company Basics

- Company name
- Website
- Headcount
- Main services or products
- Primary customer segments
- Main point of contact

## Why Now

- Why are you exploring AI right now?
- What business goals are driving this?
- What would a successful outcome look like in the next 3 to 6 months?

## Workflow Landscape

- Which teams or functions should be in scope?
- Which 3 to 5 workflows feel most painful today?
- Where is the most repetitive manual work happening?
- Where do delays, rework, or quality issues show up?

## Current Tooling

- Which AI tools are currently used, if any?
- Which automation tools are currently used, if any?
- Are there security, compliance, or data constraints we should know about?

## Economics

- Roughly how many hours per week are lost to the target workflows?
- Which workflows are most tied to revenue, margin, or customer experience?
- Are there specific KPIs you want improved?

## Stakeholders

- Who owns operations for the target workflows?
- Who needs to approve recommendations?
- Who would likely implement changes after the audit?

## Practical Constraints

- Any tools that must stay in place?
- Any budget constraints for implementation?
- Any deadlines, launches, or internal milestones affecting timing?
