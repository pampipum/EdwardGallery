# Transcript Analysis Prompt

Use this after a discovery or workflow interview to accelerate synthesis. Review and edit the output manually before sharing anything with a client.

## Prompt

```text
Analyze this business interview transcript.

Your job is to identify the highest-value AI and automation opportunities in the business based on the operational pain described.

For each opportunity, provide:
1. Workflow name
2. Current pain point
3. Suggested AI or automation approach
4. Specific tools or categories of tools that fit
5. Expected benefit
6. Estimated implementation difficulty (low / medium / high)
7. Risk level (low / medium / high)
8. Whether this should be a quick win, later roadmap item, or not recommended

Then provide:
- the top 3 opportunities overall
- the clearest quick win
- any workflows that should not be automated yet
- open questions that need validation before making recommendations

Keep the analysis practical and skeptical. Do not recommend speculative or over-engineered solutions.
```
