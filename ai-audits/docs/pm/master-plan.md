# Master Plan

## Objective

Build a Swiss-first AI audit business that can reliably generate qualified conversations, convert paid assessments, and expand into implementation work.

Primary launch market:

- Davos
- Graubuenden

Secondary expansion:

- Zurich
- Bern

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- no outreach or external communication without explicit user approval
- privacy-sensitive positioning must remain central
- Swiss tone over hype
- fixed-fee entry offer stays simple

## Core Offer Stack

### Entry

- `KI-Standortbestimmung` at `990 CHF`

### Core upsell

- `KI-Audit mit Umsetzungsfahrplan`

### Expansion

- workflow redesign
- automation setup
- knowledge systems
- implementation sprint

## Success Metrics

### Commercial

- 50 qualified target accounts
- 20 high-fit accounts manually prioritized
- 10 outreach-ready drafts prepared
- 3 approved outbound sequences ready to send
- 3 paid assessments
- 1 implementation upsell

### Delivery readiness

- sales page complete
- report template complete
- proposal template complete
- sector-specific variants complete
- lead qualification workflow complete

## Workstreams

### 1. Offer and positioning

Goal:

- sharpen the Swiss value proposition and sector proof points

### 2. Sales assets

Goal:

- produce all material needed to explain, pitch, and close the offer

### 3. Lead generation and qualification

Goal:

- create and maintain a narrow, high-fit target list

### 4. Delivery operations

Goal:

- make the assessment repeatable and premium-feeling

### 5. Founder enablement

Goal:

- support the user with presentation thinking, narrative, and buyer conversations

## Immediate Milestones

### Milestone 1: Launch-ready internal kit

Definition:

- site, report, proposal, outreach drafts, and target list are all internally ready

### Milestone 2: Approval-ready outbound pack

Definition:

- top 20 targets qualified and matched to sector-specific messages

### Milestone 3: First paid assessment

Definition:

- one approved outbound motion converted into a paid `990 CHF` engagement
