# Risk Register

## Risk 1

Name:

- weak lead quality

Impact:

- outreach approval pack becomes noisy and low-converting

Mitigation:

- tighter query terms
- manual qualification layer
- focus on fewer, higher-fit accounts

## Risk 2

Name:

- privacy messaging too vague

Impact:

- Swiss buyers lose trust early

Mitigation:

- explicit FADP / nDSG language
- careful wording on data handling, retention, and residency

## Risk 3

Name:

- offer sounds generic

Impact:

- loses to commodity AI consultants

Mitigation:

- sector-specific wording
- Davos / Graubuenden anchor
- workflow-first sales narrative

## Risk 4

Name:

- user approval dependency on outbound

Impact:

- commercial motion pauses

Mitigation:

- prepare complete outbound packs in advance
- keep everything approval-ready

## Risk 5

Name:

- overclaiming business outcomes

Impact:

- credibility loss

Mitigation:

- remove hard guarantees
- speak in realistic efficiency and prioritization language
