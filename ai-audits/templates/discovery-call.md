# Discovery Call Template

## Goal

Determine whether the prospect has enough operational pain, budget, and urgency to justify an AI audit.

## Opening

- What prompted you to look at AI right now?
- What are you hoping changes in the next 3 to 6 months?

## Business Context

- How does the business make money?
- Which teams or workflows feel the most overloaded?
- Where does work get stuck or slowed down most often?

## Current State

- Are people already using AI tools informally?
- Which tools are in use today?
- What has worked so far?
- What has created friction, risk, or disappointment?

## Pain and Impact

- Which processes are the most repetitive or time-intensive?
- Where is quality inconsistent?
- Where are margins getting squeezed?
- If nothing changes, what gets more painful over the next year?

## Buying Context

- Who would need to approve a project like this?
- Is there already budget for operational improvement or consulting?
- What timeline are you working toward?

## Qualification Signals

Good signals:

- clear workflow pain
- leadership urgency
- multiple teams affected
- willingness to share current process details
- budgeted improvement initiatives

Weak signals:

- purely curiosity-driven
- no owner
- no urgency
- wants free consulting on the call

## Close

- Based on what you shared, I’d frame this around reviewing your highest-friction workflows, scoring the best AI opportunities, and giving you a prioritized roadmap. If that’s useful, the next step is a scoped proposal.
