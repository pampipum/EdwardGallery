## Key timeline extracted
- 1927 born in Küsnacht (ZH).
- Early painting studies during school years; first independent nature studies.
- Tuberculosis diagnosis in 1947, prolonged treatment in Davos Clavadel.
- From 1949 worked in Davos; initial focus on watercolor due to studio constraints.
- 1950 early exhibition acceptance and first sale.
- 1952 close painter friendship with Gerhard Ahnfeldt.
- 1958-1960 deep contact with Albert Pfister; 1960 break and push to find own path.
- 1960-1979 dark period in oil painting with black-scale experimentation.
- 1979 heart illness followed by creative reboot with primary colors.
- From mid-1980s considered his own independent painting language established.
- 1996/97 one-year creative pause and then interior/exterior concept phase.
- 2003 Davos art commission acquired three paintings for Davos town hall.
